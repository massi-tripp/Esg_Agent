# **Taxonomy eligibility and Taxonomy alignment**

*Our declared goal is to use only recyclable and sustainable materials and to eliminate single-use plastic by 2025.*

#### Cyber security

At Mister Spex, we take cyber security as a social as well as a technical responsibility.
