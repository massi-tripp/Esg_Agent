# <span id="page-325-0"></span>**10.3. Results in 2023**

The information contained in this report considers the methodology applied by Alba and its Subsidiaries, as well as the results obtained in the second exercise performed to comply with Sustainable Finance Taxonomy Regulation 2020/852.
