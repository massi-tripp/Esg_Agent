Investments attributable to these two economic activities will be reported as taxonomy-eligible in 2023. Taxonomy conformity could not be achieved for economic activity 7.5 due to a lack of the necessary evidence of compliance with the technical evaluation criteria. A calculation of the numerator per economic activity was therefore omitted for reasons of materiality, as OpEx is immaterial for PALFINGER's business model as defined by the EU taxonomy.
